How to Use This Tool

Image Compression

  1. Upload your image by clicking or dragging and dropping
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = more compression)
  3. Select your desired output format (WebP recommended for best quality/size ratio)
  4. Optionally set a maximum width to resize your image
  5. Click "Compress Image" and wait for processing
  6. Download your optimized image

Background Removal

  1. Upload your image by clicking or dragging and dropping
  2. Click "Remove Background" and wait for processing
  3. Download your image with transparent background (PNG format)
